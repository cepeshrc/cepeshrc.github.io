What is CepeshGochi?

Cepesh Gochi is not just an extension, but your little digital companion. It lives in your browser, reacts to your actions, changes its mood, and helps you manage time in a playful way.

You can choose your favorite character from the world of games, anime, and memes to make your browser truly unique.
