Privacy Policy for Akralys

Last Updated: July 27, 2025

1. Our Commitment to Your Privacy

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Akralys: Themes & UI Styles for ChatGPT" browser extension ("we," "our," or "the extension") handles your information. Your privacy is our top priority. The extension is designed to function without collecting or transmitting your data to our servers.

2. Data Handling and User-Provided Information

We believe in transparency. While we do NOT collect, view, or transmit any of your Browse data or chat history, the extension allows you to provide information to customize your experience. It's important to understand how this information is handled.

The extension allows you to input the following types of data, which may be considered personal depending on what you provide:

  • Custom Name: You can set a custom name for the ChatGPT model display. If you enter your real name, this is personal information.
  • Custom Logo: You can upload an image file to be used as a logo. This image could be a personal photo.
  • Custom Background: You can upload an image file or provide a URL for a custom background. This image could be a personal photo.

Crucially, all data you provide is stored locally on your device and is never sent to us or any third party.

3. PDF Export Feature

To provide the PDF export functionality, the extension must read the content of your current chat conversation. This entire process occurs locally within your browser on your computer.

At no point is your chat history or the content of the generated PDF ever sent to our servers or any third party. The generated file is saved directly from your browser to your computer's download folder.

4. Local Data Storage Only

To provide a personalized experience, the extension stores all your settings and user-provided data locally on your device using your browser's built-in, secure storage API (storage.local and storage.sync). This data is under your control and never leaves your computer to be stored on our servers. This locally stored data includes:

  • Your chosen theme and custom UI colors.
  • Your user-provided data for branding (Custom Name and Logo).
  • Your custom background settings (color, URL, or uploaded image data).
  • Your layout adjustments (chat width and font size).
  • The enabled/disabled status of the extension.

5. Permissions Explained

Akralys requests the minimum permissions necessary for its functionality:

  • storage: Essential to save your settings and custom data locally and securely in your browser.
  • tabs: Used to detect when you are on a ChatGPT tab to activate the extension's features.
  • sidePanel: Allows the extension to open its user interface in the browser's side panel.
  • scripting: The core permission that allows Akralys to apply visual styles (CSS) and animations (JS) to the ChatGPT webpage.
  • Host Permissions (chat.openai.com & chatgpt.com): Required to grant the scripting permission to modify the appearance of the official ChatGPT websites.

6. Data Sharing & Third-Party Services

We do NOT share, sell, rent, or transfer any data to third parties. The extension does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

7. Your Control Over Your Data

You have complete control over your data. You can view, modify, and delete your saved settings at any time within the extension's panel. Uninstalling the extension will remove all of its locally stored data from your browser.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make any significant changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document and provide a notice on the extension's listings in the respective browser stores.
