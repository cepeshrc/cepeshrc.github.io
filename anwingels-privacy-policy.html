Privacy Policy for Anwingels

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Our Commitment to Your Privacy

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Anwingels: Premium Themes, Live Editor & Export Gemini Chat" browser extension ("we," "our," or "the extension") handles your information. Your privacy is our absolute priority. The extension is engineered with a strict "Client-Side First" architecture, meaning all visual modifications, UI editing, and PDF rendering functions operate entirely locally on your device without collecting, tracking, or transmitting your personal AI prompts to external servers.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

The Anwingels extension is built to be secure and private. We do NOT collect, view, store, or transmit your personal information, AI chat history, conversation logs, or any data you enter into Google Gemini. We do not use hidden analytics trackers or inject advertisements into your browser.

3. Theme Injection & PDF Generation

To provide its core functionality, the extension must programmatically interact with the Document Object Model (DOM) of the Google Gemini web page. This is strictly necessary for:

  • Visual Modification: Injecting custom CSS stylesheets and adjusting element properties in real-time when you use the Live Style Editor or change the Display Size.
  • PDF Exporting: The extension locates the specific chat container on the page, clones the HTML structure, and utilizes a local JavaScript library (html2pdf) to render a canvas and generate a downloadable PDF file directly in your browser's memory.

This entire process occurs strictly locally within your browser. At no point are the contents of your conversations sent to our servers for processing or rendering.

4. Local Data Storage Only

To provide a seamless workflow, the extension stores your custom preferences locally on your device using the browser's built-in secure storage API (chrome.storage). This data never leaves your computer and includes:

  • Your active selected theme and global "Extension Enabled" toggle state.
  • Your custom color palettes, background choices, and custom theme names created in the Live Editor.
  • Your ergonomic settings, including your preferred font scale (Display Size).
  • Your PDF Export settings (e.g., margins, quality presets, dark mode toggle, and preferred filename).

5. Premium Access & Third-Party Payment Processing

To unlock advanced capabilities and Platinum VIP features (such as the Live Style Editor, exclusive animated themes, and advanced PDF tuning), users can purchase a premium upgrade. We utilize a verified third-party service, ExtensionPay (which relies on Stripe infrastructure), to process all payments securely.

  • When purchasing an access tier, you interact directly with secure payment gateways hosted by our processor (extensionpay.com).
  • We do NOT collect, view, or store your sensitive financial data (such as full credit card numbers).
  • We only receive a cryptographic token confirming your active subscription status to unlock the premium features within the extension interface.

We encourage you to review the privacy policy of our payment processor: Stripe Privacy Policy.

6. Transparent Permissions Explanation

Anwingels requests only the specific permissions explicitly required for its operation. Here is the technical justification for each:

  • storage: Essential for saving your visual preferences, custom UI colors, and PDF settings locally so you don't have to re-configure them every time you open Gemini.
  • tabs & activeTab: Required temporarily to detect when you navigate to the Google Gemini website, allowing the extension to activate its features and UI.
  • sidePanel: Allows the extension to open its control dashboard in the browser's native side panel, providing a comfortable, non-intrusive workspace alongside your chat.
  • scripting: The core API required to securely inject the chosen CSS stylesheets and the local PDF rendering scripts into the active page.
  • Host Permission (https://gemini.google.com/*): Required to strictly limit the extension's scope. This ensures Anwingels only ever interacts with the official Google Gemini website and cannot access or modify any other tabs or websites you visit.

7. Data Sharing & Third-Party Services

We do not share, sell, rent, or transfer any of your data. The only exception is the non-sensitive subscription status data required for ExtensionPay to verify your Platinum VIP access (as described in Section 5). The extension contains zero third-party tracking or advertising SDKs.

8. Your Control Over Your Data

You have absolute control over your environment. You can modify, overwrite, or delete your custom themes at any time within the extension's dashboard. Uninstalling Anwingels from your browser will instantly and permanently wipe all its locally stored configurations and customizations from your device.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we introduce significant architectural changes that affect how local data is handled, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document and notify users via the extension's Chrome Web Store listing.
