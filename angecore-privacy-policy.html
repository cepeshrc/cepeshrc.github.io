Privacy Policy

For: AngeCore: Custom Themes for Gemini
Last Updated: July 8, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the "AngeCore: Custom Themes for Gemini" Chrome extension ("we," "our," or "the extension") handles your information. Your privacy is our top priority, and we are committed to being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

The AngeCore extension is designed with privacy at its core. We do NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal information, browsing data, chat history, or any user content from your Google Gemini sessions. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your IP address.
  • We do not track your browsing activity.
  • We do not read or store your chats.
  • We do not require you to create an account.

3. Local Data Storage Only

To provide a personalized experience, the extension stores all your settings locally on your device using Chrome's built-in, secure storage API. This data never leaves your computer. The locally stored data includes:

  • Your currently selected theme.
  • Your custom-created theme colors.
  • Your saved custom themes.
  • Your preferred font size.
  • The enabled/disabled status of the extension.
  • Your chosen theme for the popup window (light/dark).

4. Permissions Used

The extension requests the minimum permissions necessary for its functionality:

  • activeTab and tabs: Used to communicate with the active Google Gemini tab to apply your chosen theme and font size.
  • storage: Used exclusively to save your settings locally on your device.
  • Host Permission (gemini.google.com): Required to inject the theme's CSS styles directly into the Google Gemini webpage.

5. Data Sharing & Third-Party Services

We do NOT share, sell, rent, or transfer any data to third parties. The extension does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

6. Your Control and Rights

You have complete control over your data. You can view, modify, and delete your saved settings at any time within the extension's popup, or remove all data by uninstalling the extension.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we make any significant changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document and provide a notice in the extension's description in the Chrome Web Store.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us at: luciscepesh@gmail.com.

Summary

AngeCore is privacy-focused by design:

  • No personal data collection
  • No external data transmission
  • Local storage only
  • Minimal permissions for core functionality
  • No tracking or analytics
  • Complete user control over settings
