Privacy Policy

For: AkraGPT: Themes & UI Styles for ChatGPT
Last Updated: July 19, 2025

1. Our Privacy-First Commitment

This Privacy Policy describes how the "AkraGPT: Themes & UI Styles for ChatGPT" Chrome extension ("we," "our," or "the extension") handles your information. Your privacy is our top priority, and we are committed to being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

The AkraGPT extension is designed with privacy at its core. We do NOT collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal information, browsing data, chat history, or any user content from your ChatGPT sessions. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your IP address or location data.
  • We do not track your browsing activity.
  • We do not read or store your chats.
  • We do not require you to create an account.

3. Local Data Storage Only

To provide a personalized experience, the extension stores all your settings locally on your device using Chrome's built-in, secure storage API. This data never leaves your computer. The locally stored data includes:

  • Your currently selected theme (e.g., "cyberpunk-city").
  • Your custom background settings (color, URL, or uploaded image data).
  • Your custom-configured UI colors from the editor.
  • Your branding preferences (custom name and logo).
  • Your layout adjustments (chat width and font size).
  • The enabled/disabled status of the extension.

4. Permissions Explained

AkraGPT requests the minimum permissions necessary for its functionality:

  • storage: This permission is essential to save your settings (like your chosen theme and custom colors) locally and securely on your browser.
  • tabs: Used to detect when you are on a ChatGPT tab to ensure the extension's features are available.
  • sidePanel: Allows the extension to open its user interface in the browser's side panel for easy access to settings.
  • scripting: This is the core permission that allows AkraGPT to apply visual styles (CSS) and theme-specific animations (JS) to the ChatGPT webpage.
  • Host Permissions (chat.openai.com & chatgpt.com): Required to grant the scripting permission to modify the appearance of the official ChatGPT websites.

5. Data Sharing & Third-Party Services

We do NOT share, sell, rent, or transfer any data to third parties. The extension does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

6. Your Control and Rights

You have complete control over your data. You can view, modify, and delete your saved settings at any time within the extension's panel, or remove all data by uninstalling the extension.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we make any significant changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document and provide a notice in the extension's description in the Chrome Web Store.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us at: luciscepesh@gmail.com.

Summary

AkraGPT is privacy-focused by design:

  • No personal data collection
  • No external data transmission
  • Local storage only
  • Minimal permissions for core functionality
  • No tracking or analytics
  • Complete user control over settings
